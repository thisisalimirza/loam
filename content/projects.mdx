## Things I've Built

A mix of consumer apps, productivity tools, and experiments. Some are polished products, some are technical exercises — all of them taught me something.

---

### [SuperTasks](https://supertasks-app.vercel.app)
A task manager built around the idea that your to-do list deserves the same design attention as your inbox. Superhuman showed that productivity software could be beautiful and fast — SuperTasks applies that philosophy to tasks.

### [BuJo](https://bullet-journal-app-zeta.vercel.app)
A PWA take on analog bullet journaling, inspired by Ryder Carroll's system and the MIT Press *Analog* book. Fully local — no accounts, no sync, nothing in the cloud. The premise: the best productivity tool is the one that doesn't get in your way.

### [Raise Ready](https://www.getraiseready.com)
Practice your salary negotiation before your performance review. Raise Ready simulates the conversation so you show up prepared, not anxious. Built because most people leave money on the table not from lack of leverage, but from lack of practice.

### [MyMedStack](https://mymedstack.com)
A resource hub for anyone on the physician pathway. The real story here is on the technical side: I auto-generated 700+ SEO-optimized pages programmatically and drove over 1,000 organic visitors from Google within 5 days of launch. A hands-on lesson in how programmatic SEO actually works at scale.

### [Better Tasks](https://things-importer.vercel.app)
An ancillary app for Things 3. Take a photo of a whiteboard or handwritten task list and your items land in Things 3 — properly formatted, broken into actionable subtasks. Scratched a real itch.

### [Focus Timer](https://timer.thisisalimirza.com)
A distraction-free timer for deep work. Nothing more, nothing less.

### [Endless Idea Generator](https://thisisalimirza.github.io/endlessideas/)
A tool for breaking out of content and marketing idea blocks. Sometimes you just need a spark.
