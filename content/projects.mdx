## Things I've Built

### [Bullet Journal](https://bullet-journal-app-zeta.vercel.app)
A fully local, analog-style bullet journaling app inspired by the email client Superhuman. No accounts, no sync — everything stays on your device.

### [Raise Ready](https://www.getraiseready.com)
Practice your salary negotiation before your performance review. Raise Ready simulates the conversation so you show up prepared, not anxious.

### [MedAtlas](https://medatlas-omega.vercel.app)
A tool for navigating the medical landscape. Built to make complex information more accessible.

### [Better Tasks](https://things-importer.vercel.app)
An ancillary app for Things 3. Capture a photo of a whiteboard or task list, and your items land directly in Things 3 — properly formatted and broken into actionable subtasks.

### [Focus Timer](https://timer.thisisalimirza.com)
A simple, distraction-free timer for deep work sessions.

### [Endless Idea Generator](https://thisisalimirza.github.io/endlessideas/)
A tool for generating marketing and content ideas when you're stuck.
